Hi, I’m **CatdrinkChoco**.

This is a small project I built to help boost my productivity while keeping my health in check. Most Tabata apps don't give you the freedom to choose your own alarm sounds. That’s why I created this: a simple tool where you can attach your favorite audio tracks as your work or rest signals.

Enjoy the app, and I hope it helps you stay focused and active!

---
🚀 Key Features:
---
* **Audio Customization:** Attach any .mp3 or .wav file as your custom alarm.
* **Flexible Timer:** Set your work duration, stand duration, and total cycles.
* **Persistent Alert:** The alarm loops indefinitely until you acknowledge it—no more accidentally ignoring your breaks!
* **User-Friendly UI:** Clean, simple, and ready to use out of the box.

---
🛠️ How to Use:
---
Just build it with pygame
